### The professional credential for holistic, integrative, and intuitive practitioners worldwide.

The International Board of Healing (IBOH) provides professional credentialing for practitioners in the holistic wellness industry. The HHC designation represents a commitment to ethical integrity, professional standards, and documented practice.

## What Is the HHC?

The Holistic Healer Certified (HHC) designation is a professional credential awarded by the International Board of Healing (IBOH) to practitioners who demonstrate a commitment to ethical, client-centered holistic practice.

The HHC is not tied to any single modality. It is a cross-disciplinary credential that recognizes the practitioner's professional conduct, ethical standards, and dedication to responsible practice — regardless of whether they practice Reiki, herbalism, somatic bodywork, intuitive guidance, sound healing, or any other holistic modality.

## Who Is Eligible?

The HHC is designed for practitioners who:

• Have completed training in one or more holistic, integrative, or intuitive modalities

• Are actively practicing or preparing to practice with clients

• Are willing to operate under the IBOH Code of Ethics

• Can provide documentation of their training background and practice focus

The HHC is open to practitioners at all career stages — from newly trained practitioners seeking immediate professional credibility, to established healers looking to formalize their standing.

## What Do You Receive?

Upon successful review and approval, HHC-designated practitioners receive:

**The HHC Post-Nominal Letters**

You earn the right to use "HHC" after your name in all professional contexts (e.g., "Jane Smith, HHC").

**The Official IBOH Board-Certified Seal**

A digital badge and high-resolution seal for use on your website, social media, email signature, and printed materials.

**Global Practitioner Directory Listing**

Your verified profile is published in the IBOH Practitioner Directory, where prospective clients and referral partners can confirm your credentials and learn about your practice.

**IBOH Digital Certificate**

A professionally designed certificate confirming your HHC designation and the date of your board review.

**Access to the IBOH Professional Community**

Connect with fellow credentialed practitioners, access ongoing professional development resources, and stay informed about industry standards.

## How Much Does It Cost?

The HHC Initial Certification fee is **$350 USD**. This covers the complete board review process, all credential materials, and your first year of active registry listing.

Annual renewal fees (to maintain active status in the directory and continued use of the HHC designation) will be communicated prior to your renewal date.
